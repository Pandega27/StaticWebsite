BREMBO GP - 4RX

Brembo GP4 RX Callipers have serious high performance written all over them, from their powerful CNC machined aluminium body to their MotoGP-inspired nickel plating finish. Brembo has produced a brake calliper for the most ardent motorcycle aficionados by combining laborious design, the use of carefully chosen raw materials, and cutting-edge manufacturing technology. To guarantee that there is no extra weight without compromising the caliper's structural integrity, Brembo has gone so far as to use topological software while fabricating these callipers (RevZilla, n.d).

Compared to all OEM non-Brembo callipers, the GP4 RX has more braking force, consistency, and feel because to its four big 32mm diameter pistons. Special guides that seat each brake pad in this calliper do not require brake pad pins, making brake pad changes at home or in the pits quick and easy. Teams employing Brembo brake systems have dominated the World Superbike Championship and MotoGP circuits for years, showcasing the Italian company's technological leadership and unmatched expertise (RevZilla, n.d).
