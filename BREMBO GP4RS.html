BREMBO GP - 4RS

Brembo's very advanced casting technique was used to create the GP4-RS one-piece calliper. The result is a calliper that is 10% lighter than the renowned M4 One-Piece, which has long been a champion due to characteristics like rigidity, lightness, and accuracy (Brembo brakes, 2020).

The surrounding fins of the pistons were modified from MotoGP systems to effectively dissipate heat when braking. The exposed surface area of the fins is 30% more in callipers with fins than in callipers without fins. That difference is noteworthy given the considerable heat stress in this region and its proximity to the brake fluid (Brembo brakes, 2020).
