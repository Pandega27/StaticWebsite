BREMBO T - DRIVE

Brembo's T-Drive brake rotors are the result of years of research and development and a close working relationship with the MotoGP and WSBK. They represent a genuine advancement in braking rotor theory and design. These rotors include 8 uniquely designed T pins instead of the conventional floating button design, which secures the rotor to the carrier. This innovative design offers a full floating action in both radial and axial directions, improves heat resistance, and more efficiently transfers braking torque (RevZilla, n.d).

To keep the rotational mass of the Brembo T-Drive Rotors' carrier as low as possible, it is CNC machined from aluminium. They have a black anthracite anodized finish and laser etched Brembo emblems. For simple installation, the bolt patterns are created to match those on the OEM wheel. Teams employing Brembo brake systems have dominated the World Superbike Championship and MotoGP circuits for years, showcasing the Italian company's technological leadership and unmatched expertise (RevZilla, n.d).
