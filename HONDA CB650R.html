HONDA CB650 - R

It appears that the Japanese bike industry has discovered the secret to creating cutting-edge naked motorcycles. The only alteration to the middleweight naked in the 2023 Honda range is a colour switch since Honda has the CB650R's design and drivetrain down right (Northcott, n.d).

The light and sturdy twin-spar frame pairs perfectly with the 649cc liquid-cooled in-line four-cylinder engine. The engine has been optimised by Honda's technical staff to be fast and smooth from idle to 8000 rpm, providing a great broad powerband that is quite linear (Northcott, n.d).

While carving bends while pushing the Showa SFF-BP front suspension and looking for the bottom of the 4.1-gallon fuel tank, the comfortable seat and ideal riding posture provided by the Honda will keep you content (Northcott, n.d).
