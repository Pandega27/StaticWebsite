KAWASAKI ZX - 10R

The well-known river mark, a sign of the major Japanese bike manufacturer's continuing drive to advance, has lately been recognised by Kawasaki as their official business emblem. The first motorcycle to have the design without a supercharger was the Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R ABS sport bike in 2022, and for good reason (Miles, 2022). With its success in the most recent World Superbike championships, this potent Ninja version made a lot of people take notice, assuring that Kawis will always be considered seriously by those who crave fast speed and innovative innovation (Miles, 2022).

There are no notable upgrades over the model from the previous year in the 2022 Ninja ZX-10R ABS. 203 horsepower and 82.5 lb-ft of torque are produced by the same 998cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, 16-valve inline four engine that is currently in use. The colours for the KRT version are Lime Green / Ebony / Pearl Blizzard White as the trademarks of Kawasaki and a new colour scheme for the bike this year is metallic matte graphene steel gray/metallic diablo black, which is as futuristic and seductively wicked as it sounds (Miles, 2022).
