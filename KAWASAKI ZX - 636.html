KAWASAKI NINJA ZX - 636

The Ninja ZX - 636 2024 is a celebration of Kawasaki's flexibility. The business has never enjoyed driving in one lane. You'll like riding the newest model of this bike, a mid-size supersport, pretty much wherever that's asphalt. It seems like it was designed with the street and the track in mind.

The ZX-6R's 636cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, 16-valve inline four engine produces 52.3 lb-ft of torque at 11,000 rpm and 134 horsepower at 13,500 rpm. As rider aids, you may choose from features like an assist & slipper clutch, an economy riding indicator, Kawasaki Traction Control, variable power modes, ABS, and more (Miles,2022).

For 2024, the Ninja ZX- - 636 powertrain hasn't experienced any substantial changes, but it now ships with Bridgestone Battlax Hypersport S22 tyres as standard equipment, and the chassis has undergone minor changes to become even more slimline. The gear ratio has also been enhanced. The following livery are offered for the motorbike: The colours for the KRT version are Lime Green / Ebony / Pearl Blizzard White and; Metallic Matte Graphenesteel Gray / Metallic Diablo Black (Miles,2022).
