SC PROJECT CR - T

The CR-T silencer has always been the most recognisable exhaust designed for racing in the MotoGP and Moto2 championships among SC-Project's wide line of products (SC-Project, n.d).

The ultra-high-end finish of the outer sleeve (which comes in two variations: lightweight titanium or carbon fibre), the hand-crafted TIG weldings in a protected atmosphere, and the round and ample silencer output are a sheer delight for the appearance of the bike and for all racing-obsessed people. The titanium variant has a premium laser etched logo, while the carbon fibre version has a priceless aluminium riveted plate that matches the SC-Project Reparto Corse MotoGP and Moto2 exhaust. The CR-T's powerful and vibrant sound offers a unique sensation (SC-Project, n.d).
