SC PROJECT SC - 1R

The ground-breaking SC1-R, a standard for the Superbike World Championship, is offered with either a titanium or carbon body. The SC1-R is a high-tech silencer that was developed in response to consumer demand for a product with a racing aesthetic, but which, as a result of work by the R&D Department to produce a version that has received Euro4 approval, can be used on the road and installed on many bikes (SC-Project, n.d).

The SC1-R is the appropriate silencer for the newest super sports motorcycles thanks to its asymmetrical, angular and sleek rhomboidal design, as well as the matt carbon fibre end cap (SC-Project, n.d).

The SC1-R silencer boasts high-end technical features like titanium insert bushings that are CNC machined from solid to ensure perfect couplings, bracket and fitting welds with TIG technology, and sound-absorbing material made to withstand the extremely high temperatures and exhaust gas pressures of racing motorcycles (SC-Project, n.d).
