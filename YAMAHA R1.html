YAMAHA R1 2023

Despite being on the market for almost 23 years, the Yamaha YZF - R1 is still regarded as one of the best Japanese supersports ever produced. The 2020 Yamaha YZF - R1 has been carried over into the 2021 model, with just a few small tweaks making it, for the most part, identical to the 2020 model (Bertram, 2021).

The 998cc inline-four, which continues to produce 198 HP and 83 lb-ft of torque, has been modified to meet Euro5 emissions standards, which is the major difference between the years. This indicates that the main modifications are a slightly modified intake and a somewhat less muted exhaust (Bertram, 2021).

The standard 2023 Yamaha R1 is available in two colour schemes: Team Yamaha Blue and Performance Black (Venugopal,2023).
