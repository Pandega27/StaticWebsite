YAMAHA R6 2020

The Yamaha YZF-R6 was first released for the 1999 model year and has since gone through several iterations to become the 2020 model. It has a long history of being one of the most popularly purchased and recognisable sub-1000cc supersport bikes, and we also believe it to be one of the most attractive (Bertram, 2020).

The R6 has a history of being 'the first motorbike to' things, including the first 600cc motorcycle with over 100 HP when it was introduced in 1999. It is still one of the most potent 600s today with 117 HP in 2020. With a full wet weight of only 419 lbs, it is still among the lightest inline-4 motorcycles (Bertram, 2020).

The all new R6 also available in 3 colour, Yamaha Blue, Performance black, and Yamaha Satin Orange.
